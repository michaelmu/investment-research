### Pure Storage: AI storage throughput + data center efficiency tailwind
<span class="small">ai-beneficiary · 2026-03-09</span>

#### Pure Storage (PSTG) — AI training/inference storage “throughput + efficiency” tailwind

**Category:** Enabler (pickaxe)

##### AI tailwind thesis (mechanism)
AI workloads are storage-stress tests: GPU clusters are only as productive as the data pipeline feeding them. As AI adoption expands, enterprises/hyperscalers need **higher-throughput unstructured storage** (training data, feature stores, checkpoints) and **better $/TB with lower power/rack footprint**. Pure’s all-flash architecture (FlashBlade/FlashArray + DirectFlash + Purity) is positioned to monetize this in 2nd/3rd order ways: customers buy storage to keep AI compute utilized, to consolidate/modernize infra, and to manage energy constraints in data centers.

##### Grounding from the 10‑K (primary source)
Filing: **Form 10‑K**; **filingDate:** 2025-03-27; **reportDate:** 2025-02-02; **accession:** 0001628280-25-015044.

1) Pure explicitly calls out AI-driven demand: management lists “**increasing demand for data storage to support the acceleration in artificial intelligence (AI) adoption while managing rising energy costs**” as one of four major market trends guiding strategy (Item 1, *Business*).

2) Hyperscale wedge + efficiency claim: Pure disclosed an “**industry-first design win with Meta**” to provide flash for Meta’s storage infrastructure, citing expected benefits including freeing power/space and reducing failures vs. legacy disk (Item 1, *Business*).
