###### AI tailwind thesis (mechanism)
AI training/inference is pushing a real-world constraint: **power and heat**. As data-center density rises, operators spend more on **cooling, heat rejection, and engineered air handling** to keep uptime and energy efficiency acceptable. SPX’s HVAC segment explicitly sells **package/process cooling products and engineered air movement & handling solutions** into industrial end markets **including data centers** (Item 1). If AI capex continues, SPXC can benefit as a “2nd/3rd‑order” winner: it doesn’t sell GPUs, it sells the physical infrastructure that keeps GPU clusters operating.
