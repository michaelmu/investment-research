# United Rentals (URI) — “Data-center buildout rents” (AI beneficiary)

**Company / ticker:** United Rentals, Inc. (**URI**)  
**Category:** *Enabler* (2nd/3rd-order: equipment + temporary power/HVAC rentals that make AI infrastructure construction possible)

## Why AI progress helps (mechanism)
The AI wave is translating into real-world capex: data centers, power upgrades, substations, and adjacent industrial buildouts. URI benefits when contractors need **temporary equipment capacity** (aerial lifts, earthmoving, trench safety, fluid handling) and especially **temporary power + cooling** while facilities are built, expanded, and commissioned. URI’s scale (fleet + branch network) makes it a natural “default supplier” as projects become larger, more distributed, and schedule-critical.

**Filing metadata:** Form 10‑K **filed 2026‑01‑28**, **report date 2025‑12‑31**, accession **0001067701‑26‑000007**.

1) URI is predominantly a rental business: **$16,099m total revenues in 2025** with **equipment rental revenue = 86% of total** (Item 1, “Performance Measures” table).  
2) URI has meaningful exposure to categories directly used in data-center/infrastructure work: **Power & HVAC = 11% of equipment rental revenue** by fleet type (Item 1, “Fleet” table).  
Additional scale indicators: **Fleet OEC $22.48b**, **~1,095,000 units**, and **1,768 rental locations** (Item 1, “Fleet” and “Locations/Personnel” table).
