This is the default checklist used for daily deep dives.

## Sources
- **Primary:** company 10-K (SEC EDGAR)
- **Secondary:** investor presentations, earnings call transcripts, investor letters
- **Price context:** unadjusted OHLC daily history (trend context only)

## Deep dive template (high level)
- What the company does (plain English)
- Moat thesis (why it can sustain returns)
- Reinvestment runway (where growth comes from)
- Economics (revenue model, margins/FCF/ROIC if available)
- Risks / disconfirming evidence
- China exposure check (constraint-driven)
- Watchlist action (Add/Keep/Remove)
